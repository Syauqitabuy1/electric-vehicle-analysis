## 🔍 Key Insights
1. Tesla dominates the EV market in Washington, while Aston Martin and Maserati show minimal adoption.
2. Driving range data for 2024 and newer models is currently unavailable, as it has not yet been evaluated by the Washington State DOL.
3. Jaguar stands out with the highest average driving range across its EV lineup compared to other brands.
4. Seattle has the highest concentration of electric vehicles in Washington.
5. Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs) show higher adoption rates compared to Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs).
6. Model year 2023 represents the most widely used EVs in Washington.
